My Totally Real and Beloved Lasagna

The tomato sauce and bechamel is made from scratch, but the pasta can be boxed or pre-made just to save some time. My family has splurged over how banging of a lasagna this is, so I hope you'll enjoy making and eating this lasagna as much as I and my family have.

NOTE: There will be NO precise measurements, just approximates. Use your heart and mind.

Here is what you'll need (adjust to specific needs):

  • A house with a kitchen and stuff in said kitchen
  • A stove
  • An oven
  • A fridge with stuff in it
  • A few cans of tomato. San Marzano or other tomato variants will work. Make sure there is juice inside.
  • Tomato paste
  • 500g to 1kg of ground beef OR ground beef and pork mixture.
  • Two whole white onions
  • Some cloves of garlic
  • A mix of Italian herbs and seasonings
  • An assortment of cheeses for the bechamel
  • Some plain flour
  • Some butter
  • Some milk (preferably whole milk or semi-skimmed)
  • Some water
  • Boxed or pre-made lasagna sheets
  • And of course, some salt and pepper to taste
